About Us

Evren Education Consultants (EEC) is a unit of Evren Global Solutions Private Limited. The company is founded by a group of IT Professionals and Professors from various MNC’s and Universities across Delhi. Located in the heart of Delhi, EEC provide a complete guidance to its enrolled students including research guidance, general test guidance, Universities short listing, filling application forms and many more. Having partnership with more than 700+ Universities across 30+ countries worldwide, EEC will be the best choice for you.
