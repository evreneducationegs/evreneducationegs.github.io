Advantages of Studying MBBS In

Russia

  • The course duration of MBBS in Russia is of 6 years (internship mandatory in Russia).
  • The yearly tuition fee at top medical colleges in Russia with the low living cost is about Rs. 4.50 Lakh.
  • FMGE/NEXT coaching is available.
  • Admission is for high-quality students with 50% in PCB at 12th level.
  • Highest Indian students to study MBBS Abroad among all the countries.
  • 20:1 is the student-faculty ratio with world class infrastructure and large patients flow in Moscow.
  • All medical universities are NMC/MCI approved.
  • Rs. 20-25 Lakhs is the total budget to study MBBS in Russia in ‘A’ graded Universities.

Kazakhstan

  • The total cost of MBBS in Kazakhstan which includes food and accommodation is about 20-25 Lakhs (INR).
  • The course duration is of 5 years.
  • All Medical Universities are NMC/MCI approved and are enlisted with WDOMS (World Directory of Medical Schools).
  • Indian food is easily available everywhere.
  • Top Medical Universities have a large number of affiliated hospitals.

Enquire Now

Kyrgyzstan

  • Kyrgyzstan is the hub of medical education in Central Asia.
  • The course duration is of 6 years.
  • FMGE/NEXT coaching is available.
  • 4000+ India Medical aspirants are pursuing their MBBS with High-Quality medical education.
  • You can become a globally recognized doctor in just 20 -25 Lakhs (INR).
  • All the medical universities are approved by Medical Council of India.

General Information

  • NEET is mandatory for all medical students to apply to any medical Universities Abroad.
  • A lot of global medical exposure and international practice.
  • Access to large university campuses.
  • MCI recognized universities and colleges.
  • Better student-teacher ratio.
  • The cost of the whole course is very pocket-friendly (15-30 lakhs).
  • Faculty from all across the world.
  • Students after graduating have to qualify for the FMGE/NEXT exam to practice in India.
