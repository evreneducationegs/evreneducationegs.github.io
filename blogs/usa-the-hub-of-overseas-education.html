USA - The hub of Overseas Education

Studying abroad is undoubtedly a dream every student has seen at least once in their life. While students have a choice to study all over the world in any country possible, the place which most students commonly choose as their home for their upcoming student life tends to be, for the most record, The USA. Despite the endless options available, most international students only choose between a handful of countries with the USA as the most chosen one.

Data in 2019 revealed that nearly 5.3 million students applied for international studentship out of which more than 1.09 million chose the USA. Thus the USA has established itself as the world's most popular and influential abroad education destination. High profile prestigious institutions like Stanford University, Harvard University, Columbia University, and the University of Pennsylvania contributes to the top-rated and most distinguished establishments across the globe.

Housing nearly 5,300 colleges and universities, the USA has a record of providing the most diverse courses and programs in the world. It has become commonplace for different cultures, religions, races, and ethnicities.

As of 2019, China, India, and South Korea have sent the most number of students to the US. This enormous number of International Students in the US has created a lot of employment as well as derived a novel profession, International Education Consultancy that has till date performed a major role in the respective arena.

With such a large number of students traveling to the US from across the globe every year, it is concluded that the USA has become the ultimate hub of Overseas Education in the world.
